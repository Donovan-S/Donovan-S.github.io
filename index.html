Rapture The Flag!



Rapture the Flag is a multiplayer team-based objective game where players take control of the forces of either heaven or hell and battle it out for control of the souls of the recently departed. Teams of two battle for control of each other's soul flag in an arena filled with moving walls, spinning pillars, and the goal of the base game mode is to capture the enemy's flag while protecting yours.

The game is set to debut this year at Pax East in March! If you are attending the convention please do not hesitate to stop my booth and try it out!

Who am I?

Hello! My name is Donovan Sepulveda, and I am a game designer living in Hudson County, New Jersey. I am about to graduate from Quinnipiac University with a degree in Game Design and Development with a concentration on design. I have 4+ years of experience using the Unity Engine, with proficiency in C#, along with knowledge of Javascript, Java, C++, and HTML. I also have one year of experience working with Unreal Engine.

I have worked on a large amount of projects, ranging from serious to more light hearted projects. I am constantly looking for opportunities to innovate and create new gaming experiences! Every day is a new opportunity to be inspired and work on something new and exciting, and I take every opportunity I get to continue to learn more. Below I have listed some of the projects I've worked on, including games and art projects (both 2D and 3D).

No Place Like Home

Role: Lead Level Designer/Lead Game Designer

Project Timeline: 6 Weeks

No Place Like Home is a game that employs environmental storytelling to explore the issue of homelessness. The player takes control of a young homeless child, unravels a tragic mystery, and learns the story of how he ended up where he is today.
